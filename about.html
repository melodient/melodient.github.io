About Melodient

Melodient started as a consultancy, Melodient Limited, founded in 2013 by Dr Chris Harte and Dr Mike Terrell while working in the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science at Queen Mary University, London. The company was wound up in 2020 and the site is now the personal homepage of Dr Chris Harte.
